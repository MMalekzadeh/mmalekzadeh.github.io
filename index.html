About Me

Mohammad is currently a PhD student in Computer Science at Queen Mary University of London and a member of DataboxProject, an EPSRC project on privacy-aware personal data platform. His research focuses on privacy-preserving data mining and publishing, particularly for time-series data generated by Internet of Things (IoT) devices. Before joining QMUL, he held the position of Lecturer in Computer Engineering and led three courses at Persian Gulf University, for four s. He was also founder and director of the ICT institute at PGU for two years. Mohammad graduated from Sharif University of Technology with the MSc degree in Information Technology Engineering in the field of Computer Networks.
